About Us

SpeakAme was founded by D. Burton and T. Sanders with close consultancy from L. Jackson. SpeakAme is dedicated on providing a great SMS user experience with a focus on language translation. Both founders noticed from their travels that there is a need for communication with persons who may not speak the same language, as a result SpeakAme was born. Also, yes! SpeakAme is a mixed pun on:

  • Speak or Talk
  • “A” in Spanish means “to”
  • And “me” is obviously “me” in English

So there you have “Talk to me” or SpeakAme

  • D. Burton – Founder / Architect
    – It is great going through this journey and fulling a need that travelers like myself experienced while traveling abroad
  • T. Sanders – Co-Founder / Marketing
    – It felt good to be a part of making life easier for users who may be in a foreign land, experiencing a language barrier
