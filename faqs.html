FAQs

How do I get an account?

Download SpeakAme from your respective mobile applications market
Select “Register” and complete form to activate your SpeakAme account!

How do I delete my account?

In SpeakAme, go to the settings option. There you will find a “delete my account” option. Once selected, your SpeakAme account will be completed abolished.

Can I transfer my account to another mobile device?

Yes, login with your other mobile device. You will receive another One Time Password (OTP) to verify that you are actually making the request. Complete the verification process and your account will be activated.

If I have an issue with the app who can I contact if I have an issue?

SpeakAme’s Help feature is located under the settings option. When selected, you will be able to submit a message with attachments(i.e. screen shots) to technical support describing your issue.
Please keep in mind that, screen shots should be taken before accessing the Help feature.

Can I create groups within SpeakAme?

Yes – you can create groups in SpeakAme! Currently, there is no limit to the amount of groups that one user can create or join.

I don’t want to see the original version of the message, what do I do?

In order to turn the Translate on the Fly (TOTF) display feature off simply click the TOTF button option. (Hint: it’s the same icon as the SpeakAme logo!)
NOTE: This is simply for the display of the original message. Don’t worry about translation as it will always be active and ready to support your needs.
